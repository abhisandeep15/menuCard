Choclate Cookie

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Water

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix butter and sugars: In a large bowl, cream together the butter, sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla: Beat in the eggs, one at a time, and then add the vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  4. Cool: Let the cookies cool on the sheet for a few minutes, then transfer them to a wire rack to cool completely.
